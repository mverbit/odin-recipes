Cheese Omelette

Description

3 egg omelette filled with cheese and bacon bits

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • American Cheese
  • Bacon bits

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into mixing bowl and mix until blended.
  2. Add cooked bacon bits into egg mixture.
  3. Pour into frying pan and cook until edges solidify, then flip.
  4. Once cooked to desired consistancy, place cheese on one half and fold.
  5. Turn off stove and cover with foil to melt cheese.
