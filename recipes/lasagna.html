Lasagna

Description

3 cheese lasagna with meat and tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Rigotta cheese
  • Paramagan cheese

Steps

  1. Place first layer of lasagna noodles into baking sheet.
  2. Cover in ground beef, tomato sauce, and cheeses.
  3. Put next layer of pasta on top.
  4. Repeat until lasagna is even with top of baking sheet.
  5. Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
