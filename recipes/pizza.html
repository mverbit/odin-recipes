Pizza

Description

Plain pizza with golden brown crust

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Kneed dough and strech to desired width.
  2. Cover dough with sauce, leaving space for crust.
  3. Cover in cheese.
  4. Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
  5. Remove and let sit for 5 minutes after baking.
